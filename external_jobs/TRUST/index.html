SUCCESS Program

Design 1

This design is largely based on the previous Girls in Engineering website design. It features a carousel with space for three images, a main dock for a video to be embedded.

I've improved the design so that the top bar is transparent so as not to block the images in the carousel. Also, the colors have been slightly modified to match the Berkeley Blue featuring on other Engineering sites.

The site features a lot of "filler text" and placeholder images. That could be a downside to this design if there isn't much detail for the program as of yet.

In terms of sponsors, there are a few different places where they could go. Depending on your thoughts, I could replace the images and quotes in the bottom half of the page with sponsor information. Alternatively, they could just as easily be situated on a separate page.

Because there is currently no video for the SUCCESS program, I didn't include a video on the page, but as on the Girls in Engineering page, I can easily add one.
