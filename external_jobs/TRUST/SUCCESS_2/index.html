SUCCESS is a completely free summer program at UC Berkeley for middle-school students. The program aims to spark an interest in the joys of Computing, Engineering, and the Sciences.

Key Information

The program runs from June 23-27, 9am to 3pm.

It takes place in UC Berkeley, with local field trips.

Breakfast, lunch, and snack provided.

Free transportation available.

Application due Friday, May 8th, 2015.

We will notify you of your acceptance by May 19, 2015.
