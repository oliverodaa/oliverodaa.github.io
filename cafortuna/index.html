Ca'Fortuna

Luxurious canal-side apartment in Venice

A Home in the Heart of San Polo

Ca’Fortuna is a beautifully renovated two bedroom, two bathroom apartment on the third floor of a palazzo in San Polo in Venice.  Boasting a large kitchen, laundry facilities and spacious living and separate dining room, Ca’Fortuna is a stunning Venetian apartment which sparkles with Swarovski chandeliers and Venetian antiques as well as Natuzzi leather furniture and local artwork.  The stone balcony looks out over Rio di San Stin where gondolas glide their way along the beautiful canals.

Ca’ Fortuna has a truly tranquil neighbourhood feel, surrounded by excellent local restaurants and only metres from all amenities.  All Venice's sights are within easy walking distance or a short vaporetto ride away.

Location

San Polo is in the heart of Venice, a short walk to the train station, bus stop, Rialto Bridge and Accademia. The famous 14th century Basilica di Santa Maria Gloriosa dei Frari and the magnificent 16th century Scuola Grande di San Rocco are minutes’ walk away.
