Step 4: Pseudoinstruction Expansion

Implement write_pass_one() in src/translate.c, which should perform pseudoinstruction expansion on the load immediate (li) and branch on less than (blt) instructions. The load immediate instruction normally get expanded into an lui-ori pair. However, an optimization can be made when the immediate is small. If the immediate can fit inside the imm field of an addiu instruction, we will use an addiu instruction instead. Other assemblers may implement additional optimizations, but ours will not. For the blt instruction, use the fewest number of instructions possible. Also, make sure that your pseudoinstruction expansions do not produce any unintended side effects. You will also be performing some error checking on the pseudoinstructions (see src/translate.c for details).
